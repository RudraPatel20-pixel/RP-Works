Our Pattern Through Time

Founding

The Pattern Begins

1995

James "Pattern" Wilson, a visionary chef and pattern theorist, founded The Pattern with 12 tables in Chicago's Innovation District. Initial investment: $150,000 to create a new dining pattern.

Expansion

Pattern Recognition

2002

Received "Most Innovative Restaurant in Chicago" award, validating our commitment to culinary patterns. Expanded seating capacity and introduced our first pattern-based wine program.

Transition

New Pattern Leadership

2010

Chef Samantha Wilson, daughter of founder James, takes over as Executive Chef. Combines traditional patterns with modern techniques while evolving the family pattern legacy.

Modern Era

Today's Pattern

2023

Celebrating 28 years with sustainable patterns, 2,500-bottle pattern wine cellar, and community pattern programs. Continuing to innovate while honoring our pattern traditions.
